Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 20, 2026

See Kuan Meng ("we", "our", or "us") built Fate Reader - Card Fortune ("the App") as a free, ad-supported application. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect information when you use our App.

1. Information We Collect

We do not collect any personal information. The App does not require registration, login, or any personal data to function.

Specifically:

  • No names, email addresses, or phone numbers are collected.
  • No account creation is required.
  • Your questions and card readings are processed locally on your device and are never transmitted to any server.
  • We do not store, save, or have access to your reading history.

2. Advertising

The App uses Google AdMob to display advertisements. AdMob is a third-party advertising service provided by Google LLC.

AdMob may automatically collect certain technical information to serve relevant advertisements, including:

  • Advertising ID (a resettable, anonymous identifier)
  • IP address
  • Device information (model, operating system version)
  • General location (country/region level, not precise location)
  • App usage data (ad interactions)

This data is collected and processed by Google in accordance with their own privacy policy. We do not have access to this data.

You can opt out of personalized advertising through your device settings:

  • Android: Settings → Google → Ads → Opt out of Ads Personalization

The App offers an optional in-app purchase to remove all advertisements entirely.

3. In-App Purchases

The App offers a one-time in-app purchase ("Remove Ads") processed through the Google Play Store. All payment information is handled exclusively by Google. We do not collect, process, or store any payment or financial information.

4. Third-Party Services

The App uses the following third-party services that may collect information:

Service Purpose Privacy Policy
Google AdMob Advertising Google Privacy Policy
Google Play Services In-app purchases, app distribution Google Privacy Policy

5. Data Storage

All app data (such as your ad-free purchase status) is stored locally on your device only. No data is transmitted to or stored on external servers. If you uninstall the App, all local data is deleted.

6. Children's Privacy

The App is not directed at children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. The App's content is rated for general audiences and contains no inappropriate material for minors. If you are a parent or guardian and believe your child has provided personal information through the App, please contact us.

7. Data Security

Since we do not collect or store personal data on any servers, there is minimal risk to your personal information. All reading data is processed locally on your device and never leaves it.

8. Your Rights

Since we do not collect personal data, there is no personal data to access, modify, or delete. You can reset your Advertising ID or opt out of personalized ads through your device settings at any time. You can remove all locally stored app data by uninstalling the App.

9. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected on this page with an updated "Last updated" date. We encourage you to review this Privacy Policy periodically. Continued use of the App after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
